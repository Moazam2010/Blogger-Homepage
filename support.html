Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the software?

After downloading the installation file, simply double-click on it to start the installation process. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation. For detailed instructions, please refer to our installation guide.

What are the system requirements?

Our software runs on Windows 10/11, macOS 10.14 or later, and most Linux distributions. You'll need at least 4GB of RAM and 2GB of available storage space. For optimal performance, we recommend 8GB of RAM and an SSD drive. More details can be found on our downloads page.

How do I update to the latest version?

Our software checks for updates automatically and will notify you when a new version is available. You can also check for updates manually by going to Help > Check for Updates in the application menu. Simply follow the prompts to download and install the latest version.

I forgot my license key. How can I recover it?

If you've purchased our software and lost your license key, you can recover it by entering the email address you used during purchase in our license recovery form. We'll send you an email with your license information.

How do I uninstall the software?

On Windows, go to Settings > Apps > Apps & features, find our software in the list, and click Uninstall. On macOS, drag the application from the Applications folder to the Trash, then empty the Trash. On Linux, use your distribution's package manager to remove the software.
